About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate focusing on designing efficient algorithms with provable fairness and robustness guarantees. My research interests include social choice, mechanism design, and temporal voting models.

Currently, I work as a research engineer at A*STAR's Centre for Frontier AI Research (CFAR), where I combine neurosymbolic AI approaches to tackle VideoQA problems.
